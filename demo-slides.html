South African Healthcare Crisis

  • Doctor Shortage: 1 doctor per 4,219 people (WHO: 1:1,000)
  • Public vs Private: 84% population relies on public healthcare
  • Overworked Staff: Doctors see 40-60 patients daily
  • Admin Burden: 40% of time spent on paperwork
  • Rural Access: Limited specialists in rural areas
  • Language Barriers: 11 official languages complicate documentation

What Are SOAP Notes?

  • S - Subjective: Patient's complaints & symptoms
  • O - Objective: Vital signs, exam findings, lab results
  • A - Assessment: Diagnosis with ICD-10 codes
  • P - Plan: Treatment, medications, follow-up

Required for: Legal compliance, insurance billing, care continuity

Current Process: Handwritten → Typed → Coded → Filed (15+ min per patient)
