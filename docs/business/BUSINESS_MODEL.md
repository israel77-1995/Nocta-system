# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Segments

**Primary:**
- Private medical practices (5-20 clinicians)
- Urgent care centers
- Community health clinics

**Secondary:**
- Hospital outpatient departments
- Telemedicine providers
- Medical billing companies

**Target Market Size:**
- South Africa: ~40,000 practicing physicians
- Initial focus: General practitioners and family medicine

## Value Propositions

**For Clinicians:**
- Save 2+ hours daily on documentation
- See 20% more patients (3-4 additional per day)
- Reduce burnout from administrative tasks
- Improve documentation accuracy and completeness

**For Practices:**
- Increase revenue by $180K+ annually per clinician
- Reduce documentation errors and compliance risks
- Faster insurance claim processing (accurate ICD-10 codes)
- Better patient care coordination

**Unique Differentiators:**
- 4-agent AI architecture (Perception → Documentation → Coordination → Compliance)
- Real-time allergy and drug interaction checking
- Medical image analysis (wounds, rashes, skin conditions)
- Patient-friendly email explanations in layman's terms
- 100% LLAMA-powered (no proprietary models)
